Our Services

We Provide High Quality Services

Experience a New Dimension in Holistic Healthcare with Adarsha Hospital!

Dedicated to delivering premium health care solutions in every stage of life, Adarsha Hospital has been built for universal reach in medical services, thus is comprehensive. Explore our services below:

Infertility Care

The state-of-the-art advanced treatments in the form of IUI, cryo storage, and specialized infertility lab services aim at bringing new hope to couples in fulfilling their dreams.

Laparoscopic and Endoscopic Surgeries

Minimally invasive procedures like hysterectomy and myomectomy for faster recovery and better outcomes.

Cardiac Care

Comprehensive cardiology services, including echocardiography, treadmill testing, and emergency care for heart health.

Urology Services

Cutting-edge urological care with laser TURP, lithotripsy (ESWL), pediatric urology, and laparoscopic urology procedures.

Cancer Care

All-inclusive oncology services for prevention, diagnosis, and management of cancer diseases.

Neurology Services

Advanced treatments in neurological disorders that are supported by experienced specialists in the field of neurology with diagnostic precision.

Orthopaedic and Trauma Care

24/7 emergency services and advanced treatments for bone and joint health from fractures to joint replacement surgery.

General Surgery

Precision-driven surgical interventions for a wide range of diseases.

Pulmonology Services

Specialized care for lung health, with diagnosis and management of diseases.

Nephrology and Dialysis Services

Specialized kidney care with advanced dialysis facilities and expert nephrologists.

Fetal Medicine and Genetic Clinic

Advanced high-risk pregnancy care and genetic counseling

ENT Services

Diagnostics to the latest highly specialized surgical intervention in the management of ENT problems.

Laboratory Services

Offering fast, reliable, and accurate diagnostic support that aids in speedy and appropriate management.

Pharmacy

A well-stocked, in-house pharmacy with medicines and expert pharmaceutical guidance.

24/7 Emergency Care

Round-the-clock medical and trauma care for the immediate and effective treatment of urgent health concerns.
