About Us

Situated at OPP M G Stadium, Sharadadevinagar, Tumkur, Adarsha Hospital has been a beacon of hope for quality healthcare since 1989. Led by renowned specialists like Dr. S H Keshavamurthy and Dr. S K Harsha, we combine advanced medical technology with compassionate care to ensure our patients receive the best possible treatment.
Over the decades, Adarsha Hospital has evolved into a trusted institution, offering a wide range of specialized medical services. Our mission remains unwavering—to provide affordable, quality healthcare that meets the needs of Tumkur and its surrounding regions.
Learn More

Why Us

Our facility offers the expertise you can trust, and experienced doctors and paramedics committed to your care. Backed by state-of-the-art facilities, we ensure advanced diagnostics and modern treatments tailored to your needs. From general healthcare to the more specialized areas like urology and cancer care, we offer a holistic approach to provide comprehensive coverage of your health.
With 24/7 emergency support, we are always ready to assist in critical situations. Additionally, we provide affordable healthcare solutions, combining top-quality treatment with budget-friendly pricing, ensuring exceptional care for everyone.
